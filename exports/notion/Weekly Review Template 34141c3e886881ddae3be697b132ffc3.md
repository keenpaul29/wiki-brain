# Weekly Review Template

## 1) Capture

- Empty quick notes into `Inbox`
- Add all loose tasks

## 2) Clarify

- Convert each inbox item into next actions
- Delete or archive low-value items

## 3) Organize

- Ensure every `Task` has priority
- Keep max 3 `Active` projects

## 4) Reflect

- What moved forward this week?
- What got stuck and why?

## 5) Plan Next Week

- Choose top 3 outcomes
- Schedule deep work blocks
