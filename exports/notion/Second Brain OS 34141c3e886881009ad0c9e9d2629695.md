# Second Brain OS

A focused command center for capture, projects, knowledge, and weekly review.

## How To Use

1. Capture everything fast in `Inbox`.
2. Process inbox daily into `Projects`, `Tasks`, or `Knowledge`.
3. Review weekly and close loops.

## Areas

- Capture and Processing
- Knowledge and Notes
- Project Delivery
- GitHub Signal Tracking

## Rhythm

- Daily: Quick capture and triage
- Weekly: Prioritize and plan
- Monthly: Prune and archive
